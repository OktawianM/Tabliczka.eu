Laser engraving

Laser engraving is a very resistant marking method. It is not possible to wash off a professionally made engraving because it is embedded in the material. Another advantage of engraving is the possibility to use it on different surfaces and materials. Moreover, engraving is very elegant, which is appreciated by many of our customers. We have laser systems such as CO2, Nd:YAG, YVO and Fiber plotters and galvo type. We can also boast of the largest marking laser in Poland, which has a workspace of 2500x1300.

Advantages and features of laser engraving:
- High resistance to washing
- Can be used on many surfaces (both flat and curved) and materials (such as brass, stainless steel, aluminium, or laminate.)
- Engraving is one shade

The second technology that we mark the nameplates is UV pinting.
