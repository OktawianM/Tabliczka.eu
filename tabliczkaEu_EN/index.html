About us
Oficyna is a company with almost 35 years of experience on the market. We specialize in nameplate production using modern technology.
We make nameplates according to the customer's ready design or we design them ourselves based on the obtained guidelines. The plates are made of steel, copper, brass, bronze, laminates and aluminum. We are able to create tablets without restrictions on format, shape and design, with the possibility of personalization.
We also make according to individual needs control panels for machines. We make tables describing the functions of buttons, controls, switches and levers in all our own or entrusted material, in any shape and with any arrangement of holes.

Technology
Our company has two best technologies in industrial marking. Both laser engraving and printing are excellent for their tasks. You can find out more about them in the section about engraving or UV printing .
