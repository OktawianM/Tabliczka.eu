UV printing

UV printing is currently a very popular marking method for several reasons. Firstly, it is resistant to UV radiation (e.g. sunlight). Another advantage is the ability to print in full colour on many different materials. In addition, UV printing also allows convex printing, so it can be used for reading by blind people. The last important argument is the high resistance of printing to solvents.

Advantages and features of UV printing:
- High resistance to UV rays and solvents
- Can be used on many surfaces (both flat and curved) and materials (e.g. brass, stainless steel, aluminium, or laminate.)
- Full-colour printing possible
